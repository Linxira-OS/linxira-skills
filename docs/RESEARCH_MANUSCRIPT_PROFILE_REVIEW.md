# Research Manuscript First Batch

## Provenance Decision

The previous donor's `academic-writing` and `document-formatting` skills are
not reusable release inputs. Their resource tree has no independent license,
contains environment-specific paths and tool wiring, and includes regional and
document-formatting assumptions that do not generalize.

The donor's three top-level academic resources were traced to
`zLanqing/codex-claude-academic-skills`, whose current root is MIT at revision
`7ed6377f0efb6a38951b48ef03b19d996e454b1f`. Their donor bodies do not match
the inspected upstream bodies, so the donor copies are not republished.

The upstream `research-writing-skill` points to external reference material;
`office-academic-skill` refers to a proprietary presentation reference; and
`scientific-toolkit-skill` recursively contains proprietary, GPL, and
multi-license references. None is an approved payload body.

## First-Party Replacement

`research-manuscript-integrity` is independently written to preserve the
reusable requirement: traceable claims, verified citations, explicit inference,
and honest completion language. It does not prescribe a Chinese-only style,
regional citation format, local office installation, or a document-generation
toolchain.

The skill is a required guard for manuscript drafting, revision, review, and
rebuttal completion claims. It complements rather than duplicates the document
profiles: HTML reporting controls presentation; this skill controls evidence and
argument integrity.

## Comparison Sources

The local `open-science` research workspace is MIT but warns that nested skills
retain their own licenses. Its manuscript and literature skills remain research
references until they are added to a pinned source registry and audited
individually. The `nature-skills` workspace has no checked-out content or
license record, so it is not evidence for redistribution.

## Validation

The first-party metadata audit validates the guard. The generated payload adds
it to `core`, and lifecycle tests cover its installation through every profile.
